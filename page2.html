Inspiration

My biggest inspiration is my grandma, Sue Zipay. She played professional baseball in the All American Girls Professional Baseball League (AAGPBL) from 1953-54 before the league was dissolved.
Here is her baseball card:

She then moved to Florida and purchased a small tennis club in Englewood, the same one my family owns today and that I work at. However, she didn't stop there, as she now is pushing for the re-emergence of womens baseball in America and has joined with American Girls Baseball to make it happen. It is amazing all she has accomplished in her life and continues to do so, now at age 89.
